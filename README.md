# ObsidiAIn
 
> A fully local, privacy-first AI-powered CLI assistant for your Obsidian vault.
 
![ss](obsidiAIn.gif)
---
 
## 100% Local. 100% Private.
 
ObsidiAIn runs entirely on your machine using **Ollama**. Your notes never leave your device — no cloud, no API keys, no subscriptions. Just your vault, your hardware, and your AI.
 
---
 
## What is ObsidiAIn?
 
ObsidiAIn is a **CLI-based AI assistant** built for Obsidian users who want the power of AI without sacrificing privacy. Think of it as Notion AI — but fully local, free, and terminal-native.
 
---
 
## Features
 
### Currently Available
 
- **YouTube → Note** — Paste a YouTube video ID and ObsidiAIn fetches the transcript, processes it with AI, and converts it into a clean, structured Obsidian note
- **Summarize Note** — Select any note from your vault and get an AI-generated summary instantly
- **Interactive Note Picker** — Browse and select notes from your vault directly in the terminal using an interactive prompt
 
### Coming Soon
 
- **Note Error Checker & Improver** — Automatically scan a note for errors, reformat its structure, and improve the writing using AI
- **Web to Note** — Provide a URL and ObsidiAIn will scrape the page, extract the key content, and convert it into a clean Obsidian note using AI
